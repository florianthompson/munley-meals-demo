The latest updates to the Munley Made Meals demo — from the initial site through today's macro-targeted weekly planner.

Latest April 30, 2026

Macro-targeted weekly planner, calculator, and per-day meal slots

The single-box checkout has been replaced with a four-step wizard that builds a complete weekly meal schedule around the customer's exact daily macros. Customers can pick a goal preset or calculate their own targets from body stats and activity level.

1
Macro calculator
Mifflin-St Jeor formula with sex, age, weight, height, activity, and goal — or one-tap presets.
2
Per-day meal slots
Pick meals per day across the week instead of one undifferentiated box.
3
Volume-based pricing
Per-meal price drops automatically as weekly volume grows ($13.95 → $10.95).
Step 1Choose a preset or calculate your macros

A new entry screen lets customers either grab one of four goal presets or open a guided wizard that calculates daily targets from their body stats.

Step 1.5Daily targets, with chef-portioning context

After calculation, the customer sees their daily calorie and macro targets — calories, protein, carbs, fat — with a note that Chef Munley adjusts portions to hit them.

Step 2Set up the weekly schedule

Pick how many meals per day (2–4) and how many days per week (4–7). The price-per-meal updates live based on total weekly volume.

Step 3Fill the week, day by day

Each day shows its slots and progress at a glance. Tap a day to add meals; the active day is highlighted, completed days turn green, and the planner auto-jumps to the next empty day when one is full.

Step 4Review the week and check out

The review step lays out the entire week grouped by day, with a sticky order summary on the right that includes the customer's daily macro targets, line-item pricing, and free shipping.
